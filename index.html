Back House Operations Management

Audit Compliance & Condition Assessment Tool

1. Back House Management
1. Items are stacked departments wise
2. Every shelf has department label to identify the items.
3. Barcodes or product label is facing the front. If not then paste a barcode on the front.
4. Keep heavy products at low level for ease of collection and to prevent damage
5. Stockroom floor is kept clear and no items on floor.
6. No Trolley found with items
7. Damage items are kept clearly defined area
8. No Damage items found with good stock
9. Non Trading items are kept defined area
10. No Open pcs found with good stock
2. Damage Management
1. Damage items are segregated properly.
2. Damage segregation bins are available.
3. Damage stickers are pasted on the items.
4. No good or repairable pcs found amoung damages
5. High Value Damage items verified
3. Price Revision
1. Old prices have been removed
2. New Prices have been pasted
